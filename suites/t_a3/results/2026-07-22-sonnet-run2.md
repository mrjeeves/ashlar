## F1 persists — the known, accepted residual

`08-handle-pipe` failed again on the same misread: the reader explicitly
reasoned that "redeclaring the same named hook is a replace/override, not
an automatic chain … there is no operator or keyword here suggesting
composition (no `+=`, no call to a previous/super version), so the file-2
body is the one that wins."

This is the residual ADR-0008 accepted with eyes open: *reading* layered
`stack`/`pipe` code cold, without the reference, produces the override
model; *writing* with that model is caught at first compile by E005 with
a teaching correction. Two runs with independent readers have now
reproduced the misread exactly, and the run-2 reader's own words name the
surface signal that would close it (an explicit composition marker on
derived layers). If the residual is ever judged worth closing, that is
the design direction — recorded here as input to any future revision, not
as a decision.

Note also `stack` (06, 07: 4/4 both runs' bullets on chaining) did NOT
produce the misread — the English word "stack" itself communicates
accumulation in a way "pipe" apparently does not when encountered as a
restated declaration.
